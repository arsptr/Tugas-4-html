Dovizioso: the attendance king

The Italian,long with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage for 16 straight years - the third highest on the grid behind Valentino Rossi (Movistra Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a hug testament to the 32-year-old, who in the last two years has been - on paper - Marc Marquest' (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challanger.
