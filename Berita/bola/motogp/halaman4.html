Halaman 4 - MOTO GP

Dovizioso : the attendance king

The Italian, along with teamate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World Stage for 16 straight years - the third highest on the grid behind Valentio Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32-year-old, who is the last two years has been - on paper - Marc Marquest' (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challanger.
